喜欢的摇滚

Mar 9, 2019 | 音乐 | 阅读

一直不喜欢听摇滚的原因，一方面是嫌它太过吵闹，另一方面是见识太短，没有听到优秀的作品。

我一直不知道 Queen 是哪个乐队，甚至在第一次听《波西米亚狂想曲》的时候也没有坚持听完。直到认真的看完《波西米亚狂想曲》电影，他们所有的歌曲一下子有了意义。最近一直在听 The Battles 和 Queen 的歌曲，下面分享几首最近一直在循环的歌曲。

「Bohemian Rhapsody」(波西米亚狂想曲)

最喜欢歌曲的第二部分民谣部分，从钢琴响起脑海中就开始有了牙叔的画面，简直经典。

Mama, just killed a man,

Put a gun against his head,

Pulled my trigger, now he’s dead,

Mama, life had just begun,

But now I’ve gone and thrown it all away -

Mama, ooo,

Didn’t mean to make you cry -

If I’m not back again this time tomorrow -

Carry on, carry on, as if nothing really matters -

「Love of my love」

这首歌真对我的胃口，整首歌每句台词都喜欢。

Love of my life you’ve hurt me

You’ve broken my heart and now you leave me

Love of my life can’t you see

Bring it back bring it back

Don’t take it away from me

Because you don’t know

What it means to me

Love of my life don’t leave me

You’ve taken my love and now desert me

Love of my life can’t you see

「Oh my love」

John Lennon 的歌曲，深情演唱，很适合我的节奏，最近正在拿下这首歌曲。

Oh my love for the first time in my life

My eyes are wide and open

Oh my lover for the first time in my life

My eyes can see

I see the wind oh I see the trees

Everything is clear in my heart

I see the clouds oh I see the sky

Everything is clear in our world

Oh my love for the first time in my life

My mind is wide open

Oh my lover for the first time in my life

My mind can feel

I feel the sorrow oh I feel the dreams

Everything is clear in my heart

I feel life oh I feel love

Everything is clear in our world

「Imagine」

John Lennon 演唱的理想中的世界，理想中的生活，愿世界和平。

Imagine theres no heaven

Its easy if you try

No hell below us

Above us only sky

Imagine all the people

Living for today…

Imagine theres no countries

It isnt hard to do

Nothing to kill or die for

And no religion too

Imagine all the people

Living life in peace…

You may say Im a dreamer

But Im not the only one

I hope someday youll join us

And the world will be as one.

Imagine no possessions

I wonder if you can

No need for greed or hunger

A brotherhood of man

Imagine all the people

Sharing all the world…

You may say Im a dreamer

But Im not the only one

I hope someday youll join us

And the world will be as one.
